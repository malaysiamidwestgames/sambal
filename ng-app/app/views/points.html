Points System

Overall champion of the Malaysia Midwest Games 2015 will be the contingent with the highest total points accumulated from all events. The breakdown of points awarded for each event is as follows:-


Event Points Award Notes
Sports
Champion 50 points
First Runner-up 35 points
Second Runner-up 25 points
Performance
Champion 30 points
First Runner-up 21 points
Second Runner-up 15 points
March Pass
Champion 30 points
First Runner-up 21 points
Second Runner-up 15 points
No representative 30 points
Trash left behind 5 points per item
Volunteer
Each shift 1 points
Max points per volunteer 4 points
Max points per contingent 50 points
