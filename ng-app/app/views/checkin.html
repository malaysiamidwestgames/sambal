MMG 2015 Check In Information

In Malaysia Midwest Games 2015, Check In (formally known as registration) will be held at Mason Hall.

Participants must check in to register that they are physically here, and to collect the registration package that we have prepared for them.

Inside the registration package, you will find your name tag, shirt (if you have pre-ordered it), Ann Arbor Walking Map, Ann Arbor Visitor Guide, coupon deals and flyers to nearby restaurants.

Check in Details
Where: University of Michigan - Ann Arbor's Mason Hall Posting Wall
When: Session 1: 10am - 4pm (University of Michigan - Ann Arbor Students ONLY)
Session 2: 4pm - 10pm (Open to all except University of Michigan - Ann Arbor Students)
Parking: Street Parking on South State Street ($1.40 per hour between Mon - Sat 8am - 6pm. Free otherwise) Parking is directly outside Angell Hall Entrance (pic below).
