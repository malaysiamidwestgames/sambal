MMG 2015 Brackets Live Draw

This year we will be doing live draws for each games that will be played. The purpose of the live draw is so that each team can have a first hand knowledge about the brackets they are in and what to expect during the event itself.

We will be doing our live draw in our Twitch channel. Just click the link and watch :D.

Games that are not on the schedule are most likely either Round Robin or most Pinfall games which do not require any draws.
