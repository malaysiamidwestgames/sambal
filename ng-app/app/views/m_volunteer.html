Terms and conditions of Being an Volunteer

By agreeing to be a volunteer for Malaysia Midwest Games 2015 (MMG 2015), you are subjected to the following rules.
  1. Being volunteer will provide you the following perks:
    1. Food will be provided according to your respective shift's time (lunch or dinner)
    2. Earning points for your contingent
    3. Your personal Volunteer name tag
    4. Working for more than 5 hours will get you a free MMG 2015 Official Volunteer T-Shirt
  2. Volunteer Liability Release Form

    In consideration of my desire to serve as a volunteer in Malaysia Midwest Games to be conducted by Malaysian Student Association of University of Michigan hereby assume all responsibility for any and all risk of property damage or bodily injury that I may sustain while participating in any voluntary effort, including the use of equipment and facilities of University of Michigan - Ann Arbor.

    I currently have no known mental or physical condition that would impair my capability for full participation as intended or expected of me.

    Further, I have carefully read all the points above and understand the contents thereof and sign this release as my own, free act.

  3. You will have to pay for General Registration to be a Volunteer for MMG 2015.
  4. Volunteers will gain one point per shift, with a maximum of four points per volunteer.
  5. The maximum number of points awarded to a contingent for volunteering is 50.
  6. Volunteers must work for at least one shift.
  7. Volunteers can play sports and participate in any other MMG 2015 events, provided that they show up at their shift at the requested time. We will try our best to accommodate with schedules. However, in the unfortunate case of not being able to schedule, we expect volunteers to forgo their games or pick a replacement shift.
  8. Volunteers are responsible for their own time and belongings.
  9. Volunteers are responsible to uphold the integrity and quality that are expected in MMG 2015
  10. Volunteers are allowed to Midwest Dinner and Midwest Night, provided that they don't choose a shift that clashes with those events. We will provide the same dinner for those who worked at Midwest Dinner
  11. Failure to provide correct and current information will always revoke your volunteer status.
  12. List of current available jobs:
    1. Time Keeper
    2. Score Keeper
    3. Linesman
    4. Logistics and Set Up
    5. Check-in and Registration (must be here on Friday)
    6. Recreational activities facilitator
    7. Referees (must have licensed or our approval
    8. Game Caster
    9. Midwest Dinner Prep
    10. Midwest Dinner Server
    11. Midwest Dinner Runner
    12. Midwest Dinner Usher
    13. Midwest Dinner Clean Up
    14. Midwest Night Usher
    15. Midwest Night Backstage Helper

By click the 'Let's win my contingent some points' button, you agree on all our terms and conditions and express your understanding and agree to enter the release of liability willingly and voluntarily.
