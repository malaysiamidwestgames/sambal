Welcome

The Midwest games is an annual event hosted by one of the Malaysian Student Associations from the Midwest region of the USA with support from Education Malaysia Chicago (EMC). The event aims to gather Malaysian students from across the country representing their universities to compete in various sports and activities to claim the title of ‘Midwest Games Champion’. It also serves as a gathering point for Malaysians to meet old and new friends.

The Michigan Difference

The team, the team , the team. Michigan is all about team ethos, as championed by the legendary Bo Schembechler. It's this spirit that enables our rich tradition in academics and athletics. We Malaysians are also all about team ethos. From soccer fields to performance stages, we compete and win as a team. Come and embrace the Michigan Difference, and let's make this Midwest Games memorable for it's Malaysian Difference.

Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Q.)

When can I register for the Midwest Games, and what is the deadline for registration?

The registration period is from March 15 to April 12.

How do I register for the Midwest Games?

Create an account at our website by clicking the ‘register now’ link when it is available. Stay tuned!

Can I register if I am not a student, and do I have to be affiliated with a university to participate

Yes, you can participate as long as you are a Malaysian. No, you dont have to be affiliated with a university to participate.

Where do I stay during the Midwest Games?

We highly recommend participants to stay at one of our partner hotels. Take advantage of discounted hotel rates for the duration of the games at this link. Also, we will provide a list of accommodations for you to check out.

Would there be reimbursement for transportation?

Yes, there would be partial reimbursement for transportation

How many games can I participate in?

You can participate in any amount of games that you want as long as you be present for all of them.

Questions? Suggestions?

Email us and we will get back to you as soon as we can.
